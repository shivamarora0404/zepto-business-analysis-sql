🛒 Zepto Business Analysis using PostgreSQL








Business analysis of a Zepto product dataset using SQL in PostgreSQL.
This project explores pricing strategies, discount patterns, inventory availability, and category performance to extract actionable business insights.

📌 Project Overview

Quick commerce companies like Zepto depend heavily on data to manage product pricing, optimize inventory, and maintain efficient supply chains.

In this project, I performed a structured analysis of a Zepto product dataset using PostgreSQL.
The goal was to transform raw product data into meaningful insights that can support better business decisions.

The analysis focuses on:

Product pricing and discounts

Inventory availability

Category performance

Product value comparison

Revenue estimation

🗂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains product information available on the Zepto platform.

Table: zepto
Column	Description
sku_id	Unique product identifier
category	Product category
name	Product name
mrp	Maximum retail price
discountPercent	Discount percentage
availableQuantity	Inventory quantity
discountedSellingPrice	Final selling price
weightInGms	Product weight
outOfStock	Stock availability status
quantity	Number of product units

🔎 Project Workflow
1️⃣ Data Exploration

Initial exploration was conducted to understand the dataset.

Tasks included:

Counting total records

Viewing sample rows

Identifying missing values

Exploring product categories

Analyzing stock availability

Detecting duplicate products

2️⃣ Data Cleaning

To ensure accurate analysis, the dataset was cleaned.

Cleaning steps:

Identified products with invalid price values

Removed products where MRP = 0

Converted currency values from paisa to rupees

Standardized pricing fields

This step ensured the dataset was reliable for analysis.

📊 Business Questions Addressed

The analysis answers the following business questions:

1️⃣ Find the top 10 best-value products based on discount percentage.

2️⃣ Identify high-MRP products that are currently out of stock.

3️⃣ Calculate estimated revenue generated by each category.

4️⃣ Identify premium products with high price but low discount.

5️⃣ Determine the top 5 categories offering the highest average discounts.

6️⃣ Calculate price per gram for products above 100g to evaluate value.

7️⃣ Categorize products into Low, Medium, and Bulk weight segments.

8️⃣ Calculate the total inventory weight available per category.

📈 Key Insights

Important findings from the analysis:

✔ Certain categories contribute significantly to estimated revenue.

✔ Some high-value products are currently out of stock, indicating potential lost sales opportunities.

✔ Discount strategies vary across product categories.

✔ Price-per-gram analysis reveals which products offer the best value for customers.

✔ Inventory distribution differs across categories, affecting storage and logistics planning.
